covid 19 or coronavirus is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered corona virus
This new virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan.

symptoms
Identified symptoms are fever
tiredness
dry cough
Some patients may have aches
pains
nasal
congestion
runny nose
sore throat
diarrhea.


spread is mainly transmitted through contact with respiratory droplets rather than through the air.

There is no evidence that current medicine or drug that can prevent or cure the disease
